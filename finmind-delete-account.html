Account Deletion Request

You can delete your FinMind account and all associated personal data at any time using one of the methods below.

⚠️

This action is permanent. Deleting your account will remove all your data, including your profile, budget entries, and transaction history. This cannot be undone.

Recommended

Option 1 — Delete Inside the App

The fastest way to delete your account instantly.

1

Open the FinMind app and log in to your account.

2

Go to your Profile or Settings screen.

3

Tap "Delete Account" and confirm. Your account and all data will be deleted immediately.

Via Email

Option 2 — Request by Email

Use this if you no longer have the app installed.

Send your request to
sara288199@gmail.com
Subject line: "Account Deletion Request"
Include: The email address linked to your FinMind account
Processing time: 7–14 business days

What gets deleted

When your account is deleted, the following data is permanently removed:

Email address and profile information
Budget entries and financial records
Transaction history
Account preferences and settings
